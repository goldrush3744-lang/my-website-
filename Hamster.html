The Syrian hamster originated from Northern Syria.They were captive and used for biomedical and biology research and later bred. They are known for their stretchy and cheek pouches.Syrian hamster have a fiercely and territorial nature as they are solitary creatures that will fight,bite and hurt other hamsters.Due to this they are kept in individual cages to avoid hurting the other hamsters.
The Winter White Russian Dwarf Hamster originated from Central Asia.They were discovered by German Naturalists in 1768.The Winter White Russian Dwarf Hamster has a natural grey coat in their natural form but will turn white in the snow for survival.They are known for their vocalization as they very vocal creature and have a high pitch squeak when they are scared.The Winter White Russian Dwarf Hamster typically live for 1 to 3 years.
Campbell Russian Dwarf Hamster originated from the reigns of Central Asia,Mongolia,Tuva and Northeastern China.They were discovered by an English Explorer in 1902.Campbell Russian Dwarf Hamster was first bred in UK labs in the late 1960s.They gained popularity in the 1980s as pet.They are known for their highly socialisation as they typically live in colonies.Campbell Russian Dwarf Hamster typically live for 2 to 3 years.
Roborovski Dwarf Hamster originated from Central Asia.They were discovered by Russian Explorer in 1894.Thier name originated from the Russian explorer called Lieutenant Vsevolod Roborovski.They are known as the "robos" as they are the smallest and fastest hamster species.Roborovski Dwarf Hamster have a sandy coloured fur that is high recognised.They typically live for 2 to 4 years.
